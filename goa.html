Goan Fish Curry

Non-Veg Curry

Goa

40 mins

Ingredients

  • 500g fish (pomfret, kingfish, or mackerel)
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 4-5 dry red chilies
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 marble-sized tamarind
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup water (adjust as needed)

Instructions

1. Clean and cut the fish into pieces. Marinate with a little salt and turmeric.
2. In a pan, dry roast coriander seeds, cumin, and red chilies until fragrant.
3. Grind roasted spices with coconut, garlic, tamarind, and some water to make a smooth paste.
4. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions until translucent.
5. Add the masala paste and fry for 2–3 mins. Add water to adjust consistency.
6. Bring it to a boil, then add fish pieces gently. Cook on low heat for 8–10 minutes.
7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
