Tamarind Rice

Vegetarian

Andhra Pradesh

30 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1 tbsp chana dal
  • 1 tbsp urad dal
  • 10 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds.
2. When they splutter, add dried red chilies, chana dal, urad dal, and curry leaves.
3. Fry until golden brown.
4. Add tamarind paste and salt, cook for 2-3 minutes.
5. Mix in the cooked rice gently.
6. Garnish with coriander.
7. Serve hot.
