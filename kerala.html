Avial

Side Dish

Kerala

45 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, yam, raw banana, drumstick)
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 cup curd (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • A few curry leaves

Instructions

1. Chop all vegetables into long pieces and cook them with turmeric and salt until tender.
2. Grind coconut, green chilies, and cumin to a coarse paste.
3. Add the coconut paste to the cooked vegetables and mix well. Cook for 2â€“3 minutes.
4. Add curd (if using) and stir gently. Do not boil after adding curd.
5. Drizzle coconut oil and add curry leaves.
6. Serve hot with rice.
