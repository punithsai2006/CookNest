Phagshapa

Non-Vegetarian

Sikkim

60 mins

Ingredients

  • 500g pork belly, cut into strips
  • 250g radish, sliced
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, julienned
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper powder
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

1. Clean and cut pork belly into thin strips.
2. In a pot, add pork and water. Bring to boil.
3. Add sliced radish, dried chilies, garlic, and ginger.
4. Simmer on medium heat for 30-35 minutes.
5. Add sliced onions and cook for another 10 minutes.
6. Season with salt and black pepper.
7. Cook until pork is tender and radish is soft.
8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice.
