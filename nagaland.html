Smoked Pork

Non-Vegetarian

Nagaland

1 hr

Ingredients

  • 500g smoked pork, cut into chunks
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Instructions

1. Heat mustard oil in a pot.
2. Add onions and garlic; saut√© until golden brown.
3. Add smoked pork chunks and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
4. Add green chilies, salt, and enough water to cover the pork.
5. Simmer on low heat for about 40 minutes or until tender.
6. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired and serve hot with rice.
