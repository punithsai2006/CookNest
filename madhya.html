Bhutte ka Kees

Snack

Madhya Pradesh

30 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated corn (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp grated ginger
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • Grated coconut (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

1. Heat ghee in a pan, add mustard and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
2. Add green chilies, ginger, and turmeric. Sauté for a few seconds.
3. Add grated corn and cook for 5–7 minutes, stirring continuously.
4. Pour in milk and cook until the mixture thickens and milk is absorbed.
5. Add salt and mix well. Cook for another 2–3 minutes.
6. Garnish with chopped coriander and grated coconut.
7. Serve hot as a healthy snack.
