Thukpa

Non-Vegetarian

Arunachal Pradesh

45 mins

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken (boiled and shredded)
  • 1 packet noodles
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • 1/2 cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups chicken broth or water
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

1. Boil noodles and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions, ginger-garlic paste until golden.
3. Add chopped vegetables and cook till soft.
4. Add tomato, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook until tomatoes are mushy.
5. Add chicken broth and shredded chicken. Simmer for 10 mins.
6. Add boiled noodles and mix well. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
