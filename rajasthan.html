Dal Baati Churma

Vegetarian

Rajasthan

90 mins

Ingredients

  • For Baati:
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup semolina
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • For Dal:
  • 1 cup toor dal
  • 1/2 cup chana dal
  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Spices (turmeric, red chili, garam masala)
  • For Churma:
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • Cardamom powder

Instructions

1. For Baati: Mix flour, semolina, salt, and ghee. Make firm dough.
2. Shape into balls, bake at 180°C for 30-35 minutes.
3. For Dal: Cook all dals together with turmeric.
4. Prepare tempering with ghee, cumin, onions, tomatoes, and spices.
5. Add tempering to dal and simmer.
6. For Churma: Roast wheat flour balls till golden.
7. Grind with sugar and cardamom.
8. Serve hot baatis with dal and churma, drizzled with ghee.
