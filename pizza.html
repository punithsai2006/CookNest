Chicken Pizza

Fast Food

Italy/Global

1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup cooked chicken (shredded/grilled)
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • 1/4 cup onions (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup capsicum (sliced)
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 220°C (428°F).
2. Spread pizza sauce over the base evenly.
3. Add shredded chicken, onions, capsicum, and cheese.
4. Sprinkle oregano, chili flakes, salt, and pepper.
5. Drizzle olive oil over the top.
6. Bake in preheated oven for 12–15 minutes or until cheese is melted and crust is golden.
7. Slice and serve hot with ketchup or garlic dip.
