Hyderabadi Biryani

Non-Vegetarian

Telangana

120 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Basmati rice
  • 1 kg mutton, cut into pieces
  • 4 onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cups yogurt
  • 4 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 4-5 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 cup mint leaves
  • 1 cup coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 cup oil
  • Saffron soaked in milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cinnamon, cloves)

Instructions

1. Marinate mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for 2 hours.
2. Cook rice with whole spices until 70% done.
3. In a heavy-bottomed pan, fry onions until golden brown.
4. Layer half the rice, add marinated mutton, top with remaining rice.
5. Add saffron milk, mint, coriander leaves.
6. Seal the pot with dough and cook on low heat for 45 minutes.
7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before opening.
8. Serve hot with raita and salan.
