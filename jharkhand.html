Thekua

Dessert / Snack

Jharkhand

30 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup grated jaggery or sugar
  • 2 tbsp grated coconut (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Water (as required)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, add flour, jaggery (or sugar), coconut, cardamom powder, and ghee.
2. Mix everything well and add water little by little to form a firm dough.
3. Divide into small balls and flatten each into a round or oval shape.
4. Design with a fork or Thekua mold (optional).
5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the Thekua on medium flame until golden brown.
6. Drain excess oil and allow to cool. Serve as a snack or sweet treat.

Cooking Tips

Make sure the oil is medium hot; too hot oil will burn the outer layer quickly.

Using jaggery gives an authentic flavor and adds softness to Thekua.
