Mui Borok

Traditional

Tripura

40 mins

Ingredients

  • 500g bamboo shoots
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 4-5 green chilies
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds

Instructions

1. Clean and chop bamboo shoots into small pieces.
2. Heat mustard oil, add cumin seeds and bay leaves.
3. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies.
5. Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, and salt.
6. Add bamboo shoots and cook for 20-25 minutes.
7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
