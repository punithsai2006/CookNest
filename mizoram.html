Bai

Vegetarian

Mizoram

30 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup spinach (or other green leaves)
  • 1/2 cup beans
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin (cubed)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
2. In a pot, add all ingredients with enough water to cover the vegetables.
3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20-25 minutes until vegetables are tender.
4. Adjust salt and serve hot with rice.
Bai is a simple, healthy stew often enjoyed in Mizoram households.
